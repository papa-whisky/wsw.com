Date, Time, and Location

Ceremony and reception at The Burrow in Denver, on Saturday 11th December, 2021 at 4pm (please arrive at 3:30pm).

Where the heck is Denver?

Great question. Denver is a locality between Daylesford and Kyneton, about 100kms Northwest of Melbourne.
Clare grew up in Denver, and her parents lived there from 1986 until 2015. There are no shops, pubs or public transport. However, there are thousands of kangaroos, stunning bushland and, since 2017, a wedding venue, The Burrow.

Transportation

A complimentary bus will take guests between Daylesford and The Burrow. The bus will depart from the Daylesford Post Office at 3.00pm. The bus will leave The Burrow at 11pm and return guests to the Daylesford Post Office.
If you are staying at Jubilee Lake Holiday Park, please let us know, so we can arrange for the bus to drop you off.
